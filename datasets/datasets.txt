Human datasets:

We combine five publicly available labeled facial expression databases to create the HED:

 (a) Static Facial Expressions in the Wild (SFEW) database 

(b) Extended Cohn-Kanade database (CK+) 

(c) MMI database 

(d) Karolinska Directed Emotional Faces (KDEF)

(e) Denver Intensity of Spontaneous Facial Actions (DISFA) database
